Our Solution: VitaScroll

AI-powered healthy Tinder for news: Bite-sized, personalized content in native languages with mindful swiping mechanics.

What is it?

An AI-powered platform delivering bite-sized, personalized news in native languages through mindful swiping mechanics, transforming doom scrolling into empowering sessions.

Key Advantages

Reduces unhealthy doom scrolling while boosting awareness with reliable, personalized feeds and native language support for 7.1M HK users.

Target Users

HK young adults aged 18-35 (2.5M segment), including urban professionals and students seeking quick, positive news updates.

How It Works

  • AI condenses news into 50-word cards.
  • Genre selection for tailored content.
  • Swipe mechanics for intuitive navigation.
  • Occasional surprise coupon ads for engagement.

Key Functionality

Double-Tap for In-Depth Reading

Double-tap a news card to expand into a detailed thread with more images, context, and full article insights.

Swipe Mechanics

Swipe right if interested (save/bookmark), left if uninterested (skip and refine feed).
